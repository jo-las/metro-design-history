## Key Characteristics Observed

- **Typography-first design** with dramatically large headings
- **Bold, flat colors** (Microsoft Blue, Magenta, Teal, Lime)
- **Tile-based layouts** with live tiles of varying sizes
- **Clean sans-serif typography** (Segoe UI)
- **High contrast** white text on colored backgrounds
- **Minimal ornamentation** - content before chrome
- **Grid-aligned** strict layouts
- **Information density** - efficient use of space
